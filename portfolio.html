2. Poster Designs

Poster 1



Curtain Call is a conceptual poster series for an original play about a theatre preparing for its final performance, and the haunting secrets behind its closure. The story is told through the perspective of a devoted actress obsessed with perfecting her craft, blending themes of ambition, obsession and decay.



Poster 3



Adobe Illustrator was used for illustration and layout, while Photoshop was used for mockups and presentation. The fonts used in this project are Runavess and Poppins.

I created a set of three vector-style illustrations using Adobe Illustrator and used Photoshop for final mockups and presentation. The posters feature a dark, moody colour palette to evoke suspense and melancholy, visually capturing the play’s emotional tension and theatrical atmosphere.



Poster 2

3. Self Portrait

MY PREVIOUS PERLER BEAD ART

Over four weeks, I individually placed over 2,000 beads by hand, carefully counting and arranging each colour to form the portrait.

Reference to physical piece

This project strengthened my skills in beadwork, pixel art, colour harmony, stylisation, and material handling.

This project involved creating a self-portrait using Perler beads, small, colourful plastic beads that can be arranged on pegboards and fused together.

Process

I stylised a photograph of myself through Photoshop, then used Krita to draw bright colours over the distinctive shadows and highlights.

4. Multimedia

Soft pastel daisies









A portrait of a laughing princess made using coloured pencils and water colours. This was a portrait study & I wanted to replicate the painting style of old Victorian Era paintings. This project taught me shading and proportions.








Apple study







A portrait of Captain Jack Sparrow, created in the style of the artist Bisa Butler. Acrylic on canvas, This project was my first time using acrylics and it taught me colour harmony and anatomy.








Chess Painting






A scaled down version of the world map made using Lego studs. Each dot is an individual piece of plastic placed one by one on a huge grid. This project taught me patience above all, along with perspective and layouting.








Ducks in the sun










A nature study of daisies I made using water colours and soft pastels on watercolour paper. This project taught me realism and how to work with 2 almost fundamentally different art mediums.








colour pencil portrait








A hyper-realistic drawing of an apple under running water, made entirely with coloured pencils. This project took months to complete and taught me rendering, light and shadow and composition.









Canvas painting of jack sparrow










A 3x4 feet painting, acrylic on canvas as a personal project for my living room. My family enjoys playing chess, so I decided to commission myself this painting. It taught me composition hierarchy and perspective.









lego world map









A landscape illustration of ducks bathing in sunlight, made using soft pastels on water colour paper. This was made to study light and shadows. It taught me animal anatomy, colour theory and shading.
